# RouterGPT User Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you use our Custom GPT to find and book services at nearby businesses.

---

## What is RouterGPT?

RouterGPT is an AI-powered assistant that helps you:
- **Find nearby businesses** based on your location
- **Get personalized recommendations** based on your needs
- **Book appointments** directly through chat
- **Receive real-time availability** and pricing

All in natural conversation with ChatGPT!

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Share Your Location

When you start a conversation, just tell RouterGPT where you are:

**Examples:**
- "I'm in Phoenix, AZ and need a haircut"
- "Find me a barber near 85281"
- "I'm at 123 Main St, Tempe and need a stylist"
- "I'm looking for a salon near Arizona State University"

You can share your location in several ways:
- **City and State**: "Phoenix, AZ"
- **ZIP Code**: "85281"
- **Address**: "123 Main St, Tempe, AZ"
- **Landmark**: "Near Arizona State University"

> **Privacy Note**: RouterGPT only uses your location to find nearby businesses. Your location is not stored permanently.

### Step 2: Specify What You Need

Tell RouterGPT what service you're looking for:

**Examples:**
- "I need a men's haircut"
- "Looking for balayage highlights"
- "Need a beard trim"
- "Want to get my nails done"
- "Looking for a deep tissue massage"

You can also add preferences:
- "I prefer somewhere that takes walk-ins"
- "Looking for a place open on Sundays"
- "Need somewhere that specializes in curly hair"
- "Want a barber with experience in fades"

### Step 3: Choose a Business

RouterGPT will show you nearby options with:
- **Business name** and location
- **Distance** from you
- **Specialties** and services
- **Hours of operation**
- **Contact information**

Example response:
```
I found 3 barbershops near you:

1. **Bishop's Barbershop** (1.2 miles away)
   - Specializes in: Classic cuts, fades, beard trims
   - Hours: Mon-Sat 9am-7pm, Sun 10am-5pm
   - 480-555-1234

2. **The Cutting Edge** (2.5 miles away)
   - Specializes in: Modern styles, straight razor shaves
   - Hours: Tue-Sat 10am-8pm
   - 480-555-5678

3. **Tempe Hair Studio** (3.8 miles away)
   - Specializes in: All hair types, color services
   - Hours: Mon-Sat 9am-6pm
   - 480-555-9012

Which one interests you?
```

### Step 4: Book Your Appointment

Once you select a business, RouterGPT will:
1. **Connect you directly** to that business's booking system
2. **Ask for your details** (name, phone, email)
3. **Show available times**
4. **Confirm your booking**

The entire conversation stays in ChatGPT - no need to switch apps!

## Common Questions

### How accurate is the location search?

RouterGPT searches within a radius you can control:
- Default: **10 miles**
- You can request: "Search within 5 miles" or "Expand search to 20 miles"

Distances are calculated as straight-line distances and are approximate.

### What if I don't find what I'm looking for?

Try these tips:
- **Expand your search radius**: "Can you search within 20 miles?"
- **Be more specific**: Instead of "haircut", try "barber specializing in fades"
- **Try a different location**: "What about in downtown Phoenix?"
- **Ask for alternatives**: "Are there any salons that do men's cuts?"

### Can I see all details about a business?

Yes! Once you select a business, RouterGPT provides:
- Full address
- Phone number
- Website (if available)
- Complete service menu
- Pricing information
- Staff bios
- Customer reviews (if available)

### What information do I need to share?

For **searching**: Just your location (city, ZIP, or address)

For **booking**: 
- Your name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Preferred date/time
- Service needed

### Is my information secure?

Yes! RouterGPT:
- Only uses your location to find nearby businesses
- Shares your contact info only with the business you choose to book with
- Doesn't store your location permanently
- Follows ChatGPT's privacy policies

See our [Privacy Policy](#privacy-policy) below.

### Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes! After booking, you'll receive:
- **Confirmation email** with booking details
- **Business contact info** to reschedule
- **Cancellation instructions**

You can also ask RouterGPT: "Can you help me reschedule my appointment at [Business Name]?"

### What if the business is closed?

RouterGPT checks business hours and will:
- Show only open businesses (if you need same-day service)
- Indicate closed days
- Suggest alternative times

### Can I book multiple services?

Yes! Just tell RouterGPT:
- "I need a haircut and beard trim"
- "I want highlights and a cut"
- "Book me for massage and facial"

RouterGPT will find businesses that offer all requested services.

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't find any businesses near you"

**Solutions:**
1. Expand your search radius: "Search within 25 miles"
2. Try a broader service type: "Any hair salons?" instead of "Balayage specialists"
3. Try a nearby city: "What about in Scottsdale instead?"

### "The times shown don't work for me"

**Solutions:**
1. Ask for different days: "What about next week?"
2. Request specific times: "Do they have early morning or evening slots?"
3. Ask about waitlist: "Can I be added to a cancellation list?"

### "I selected the wrong business"

**Solution:**
Just say: "Actually, I want to go back and choose a different business"

RouterGPT will show you the list again.

### "The conversation got confused"

**Solution:**
Start fresh: "Let's start over. I need to find a [service] in [location]"
